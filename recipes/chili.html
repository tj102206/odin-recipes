Chili

Description

Look at this tasty ass bowl of chili. Warms your heart on a cold night

This recipe was passed down from generations of vikings.

Ingredients

  • 1lb Turkey Meat
  • 1 Onion
  • 2 Cans Diced Tomato
  • 1 Green Pepper
  • 1 Red Pepper
  • 2 Cans of red kidney beans
  • Salt, pepper, sour cream

Steps

  1. Cook ground turkey & onion
  2. Add cans of diced tomato to pot
  3. Add meat to pot
  4. Add spices & beans
  5. Let simmer for 5 hours
  6. Enjoy
