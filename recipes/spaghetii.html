Poor Man Pasta

Description

This is the most basic spaghetti receipe you'll find. It can barely be called pasta.

Ingredients

  • Can of Tomato Sauce
  • spaghetti noodles
  • Salt
  • 1/2 lb of hamburger or sausage
  • Garlic if you fancy

Steps

  1. Boil noodles
  2. Cook meat
  3. Add sauce
  4. Add salt or garlic until its edible
